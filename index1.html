Your Basic Rights in India 🇮🇳

  • ⚖ Right to Equality: No discrimination based on religion, race, or gender.
  • 🗽 Right to Freedom: Speak freely, protest peacefully, and choose your work.
  • 🚫 Right Against Exploitation: No forced labor or child labor.
  • 🕌 Right to Freedom of Religion: Practice any religion you like.
  • 🎓 Right to Education: Free education for kids aged 6-14 years.
  • ⚖ Right to Constitutional Remedies: Go to court if your rights are violated.
  • 👩⚖ Right to Privacy: No one can spy on you or your messages.
  • 🍲 Right to Food: No one should starve (government gives food subsidies).
  • 🌳 Right to a Clean Environment: Clean air and water for everyone.
  • 🏥 Right to Healthcare: Hospitals must help in emergencies.
